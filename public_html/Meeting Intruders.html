Meeting Intruders

A chrome extension to make your never-ending meetings a little more fun!

Meetings: Useful? Absolutely. Boring? Wellll, meetings are rarely the highlight of anyone’s day. And that was before 2020 kicked in, turning meetings into a nonstop Zoom-athon of home-based enterprise.

Meeting intruders is a fun, private, distracting escape that lets you grin your way through meetings and leaves your teammates none the wiser.
As soon as you start, you’ll know what to do.

Try it out on the Google Play Store
