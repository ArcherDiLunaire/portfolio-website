Junger Kammerchor

A modern dynamic identity for a young classical choir

We proposed a sound reactive logo that responds to vocal and musical interaction by moving like notes on a scale.

We used a sophisticated and minimalistic approach for the design, achieved through an elegant black & white color palette. This helps create a bold, identifiable brand whilst facilitating usability and making promotional material convinient to produce.
