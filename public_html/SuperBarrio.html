SuperBarrio

A videogame for people to participate in designing their neighbourhood

Superbarrio is a game designed to boost participatory design. It makes use gamification strategies to engage the citizens in the design of their own public space.

As an open-source tool, Superbarrio widens the potential audience of participatory design processes, overcoming the limits of conventional methodologies.

The first case study was the Superblock of Barcelona. The game was tested in a launching event in March 2017 with citizens who resided or worked in the neighborhood.

In the different tests on site, it was possible to observe a significant advancement in the behaviour of the users in relation to the effort of keeping their neighborhood balanced.
The various consequences of each design decision, vehiculated by the specific gaming strategies, had a clear influence on the decision making process of the user. Highlighting the potential of such interface as a powerful educational tool.

Created in collaboration with the students of the Master in Cities and Technology

SuperBarrio is a project of IaaC, Institute for Advanced Architecture of Catalonia at Master in City and Technology in 2016/2017
