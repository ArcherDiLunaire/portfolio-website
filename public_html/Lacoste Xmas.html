Lacoste Xmas

A fun and innovative solution to a christmas packaging design for Lacoste.

The design revolves around 2 predominant caracteristics: The playful interaction of opening the christmas box to reveal the emblematic Lacoste crocodile, and the elegance of the petit-piqué wrapping of the box and its red silk interior.

Awarded Runner Up at LACOSTExCSM exhibition
