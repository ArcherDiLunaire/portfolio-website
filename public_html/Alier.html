Alier

A gamified experience to promote Alier: a paper recycling company

This simple and intuitive interactive game was created to help the visitors understand the various services & products of the company. Using the Leap motion, a hand tracking device, helped engage the attendees and make the game more easily accessible and fun.

Exhibited at the Global Gypsum Conference 2022, Lisbon.
