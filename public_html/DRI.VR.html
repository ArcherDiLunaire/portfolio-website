DRI.VR

The VR experience that enables you to be at the wheel of a toy remote control car.

Virtual Reality offers the possibility to access the macroscopic and the microscopic to see and feel things in ways we’ve never done before.

DRI.VR takes advantage of this in a fun and playful way, offering an experience that will change your perspective and really make you feel small.

Exhibited at the Central Saint Martins Degree Show 2015, London.
