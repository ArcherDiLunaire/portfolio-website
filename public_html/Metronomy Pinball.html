Metronomy Pinball

The retro Pinball Machine that recreates a Metronomy tune.

Inspired by the retro feel of the Metronomy album: Summer 08, this custom designed pinball machine has each of its bumpers playing a stem from the hit song: Old Skool.

As they play the game, each player creates their own version of the Metronomy tune!

Created in Collaboration with Persiis Hajiyanni
Sound Design by Stefan Iyapah

Exhibited at the "Metronomy - Summer 08 " album launch - Sonos Studios, London.
